Overview

International School Of Photonics (ISP) which came into existence in 1995 has research activities including the design and fabrication of various laser systems and laser components and detailed investigations of non linear phenomena in different materials using photo thermal, photo-acoustic and related techniques, time and space resolved studies on laser induced plasmas from a variety of targets, design and characterization of fiber optic sensors, fabrication and characterization of polymer fibers, preparation and investigation of photonic materials and studies on photonics theory. ISP has collaboration and interaction with institutions like Eindhoven University of Technology, The Netherlands; Sultan Quaboos University in Oman, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore etc.

The newly established IEEE Photonics society aims to aid the students in amassing a plethora of rela world skills and intra and extra curricular soft skills. Albeit being in its infant stages, The society has been forseeing the impplementation of ISP's second flagship programme, "Optics to school", Whos primary aim is to introduce optics and a career in photonics to young schoolgoers. You can find more details in the activities tab.

Activities

Optics to school

Optics to school is an outreach programme aiming to familiarise the general population about the wonders of optics and photonics. Owing to its novelty, Photonics is still largely unfamiliar among the common populace and this is exactly what Optics to School is set to tackle. The target audience for the programme currently holds high school students as well as general school children fascinated by the wonders of light.

The programme currently proposes to enlighten the students on key concepts that outline the field of photonics such as

  • Reflection - Refraction
  • Interference
  • Diffraction
  • Polarisation
We aim to connect the above basic concepts to its real world application such as fibre optics, Holography and even something as commonplace as sunglasses. The programme offers young students a chance to tap into thier dormant feelings of scientific aptitude and is sure to get them knee-deep into the wonderful world of light, matter and much more!
